How to use Donna

Plain-English answers. Find what you're stuck on, then get back to work.

How do I create an estimate?

Type "estimate for [customer name]" into the chat, or tap + → New Estimate. Donna pulls your labor rate, markup, and tax settings from your profile automatically. Review the draft on the right, then approve to send.

How do I send an invoice?

Same as estimates — type "invoice [customer]" or tap + → New Invoice. Donna pulls the job, calculates totals, and drafts the email. If QuickBooks is connected, it syncs there too.

What's the difference between a Project and a Job?

Job = a single visit / a single ticket. One day of work for one customer.
Project = a bigger build that has multiple jobs under it. Example: "Smith Kitchen Remodel" is a project; "Demo day," "Cabinet install," and "Final paint" are jobs inside it.
Most simple service work is just jobs — you only need Projects when one customer's work spans multiple visits.

How do I add a customer?

Three ways: tell Donna in chat ("add customer Jane Smith, 555-1212"), tap + → New Customer, or go to My Customers and tap + Add Customer. Anything Donna drafts for a new customer name auto-creates the record.

How do I connect QuickBooks?

Bottom-left sidebar → Setup Connections → QuickBooks Online. You'll sign in to Intuit once and Donna will sync estimates, invoices, and receipts after that. One connection covers both Operations and Finance.

Does anything send without me seeing it?

No. Donna drafts — you approve. Every estimate, invoice, email, and calendar event waits in the right-hand panel until you tap Approve. Receipt uploads and customer adds happen instantly because they're not communications.

Why is the connection dot red?

Donna only shows green when she's actually verified the connection against your account. Red means either you haven't set it up yet or the token expired — tap Setup Connections to reconnect. She'll never fake a green light.

Something broke. What now?

Email support@bedrock-digital.com with what you were doing when it broke. Brock reads everything that comes through — usually same-day fix.
