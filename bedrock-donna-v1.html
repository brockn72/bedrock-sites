Meet Donna

A few quick questions — then Donna's ready.

Donna is your operational admin secretary — she drafts estimates, sends invoices, uploads receipts, schedules jobs, and keeps your customer records organized. Sassy, smart, and always ready. You approve everything before it goes anywhere.

Everything you share with Donna stays completely private. Your rates, markups, and preferences are never shared, sold, or visible to anyone outside your account. Donna uses this information only to help build accurate estimates and invoices for your business.

How to use Donna

The short version — you'll get the hang of it fast.

Just talk to her

Type or speak what you need in plain words — or tap the + button under the chat to start a workflow. Donna asks who it's for, then drafts it.

You approve everything

Donna only drafts. Every estimate, invoice, email, and calendar event waits in the right-hand panel for your Approve.

Everything's linked

Customers, projects, jobs, and notes all connect. Add a job and it rolls up under a customer and a project automatically.
